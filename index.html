Official Privacy Policy & Information Page

Introduction

Torque Legends is designed for a general audience and provides a realistic racing and driving experience for Android users.

We respect user privacy and aim to comply with all applicable policies and regulations including Google Play policies.

Information We Collect

We do not collect personal information such as real names, addresses, or passwords.

Some non-personal information such as device performance, crash logs, and gameplay statistics may be collected to improve game stability and performance.

Advertisements

Torque Legends uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. Some ads may be rewarded ads that provide optional in-game rewards.

Ad services may collect limited device-related data according to Google's advertising policies.

Children's Privacy

Torque Legends does not knowingly collect personal data from children under the age of 13.

If any such information is discovered unintentionally, it will be removed immediately.

About Torque Legends

Torque Legends is a realistic driving and racing game developed by Neuroplayy Technologies.

The game focuses on immersive gameplay, realistic handling, vehicle customization, and optimized performance for Android devices.

Game Features

  • Realistic driving physics
  • Vehicle customization system
  • Open-world environments
  • Drift and race tracks
  • Reward systems and bonuses
  • Optimized graphics settings
  • Garage and vehicle selection systems

Community Guidelines

Players are expected to use the game fairly and respectfully.

Exploiting bugs, modifying files, cheating, or abusing systems may result in restrictions in future online features.

Future Updates

Future updates may include:

  • More vehicles
  • Expanded maps
  • Better graphics
  • Optimization improvements
  • New game modes
